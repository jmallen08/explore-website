About Us

It's Nice to Meet You!

Our application, Explore!, drastically changes how K-12 students learn concepts and build skills by integrating augmented reality, inquiry-based learning, state standards, and gamification. With our product, students can touch, move, and experiment with a concept and practice skills, through the iPads, Chromebooks, and laptops schools have already purchased. The combination of these features is a revolutionary approach that can change the way students use technology to understand concepts in math and science.

Our Team

Wesley Virt Chief Executive Officer & Co-Founder

Wesley’s main responsibility insures Explore Interactive creates value for students, administrators, and teachers from across the nation. To accomplish this goal, Wesley has interviewed over 75 teachers and administrators from across the nation and extensively researched the best pedagogical approaches to include in Explore!’s missions.

Alex Bussan Chief Technical Officer & Co-Founder

With extensive knowledge in augmented reality technology and various coding languages, Alex directs the software development team. As a Co-Founder, Alex is constantly looking for new insights on how to solve the needs of teachers through engaging technology.

Chris Palermo Marketing Associate

Hailing from Silicon Valley, Chris has an infectious energy and passion for innovation. His role within Explore Interactive involves marketing Explore! to parents, teachers, and administrators.

Darlene Seifert Advisor

Darlene, a 25-year Biology teacher at New Palestine High School, reviews scientific content to ensure we are capturing the essence of the experiment.

Dr. Amanda Thompson Advisor

As a Corporate Finance Professor and experienced entrepreneur, Dr. Thompson oversees the strategic and financial planning within Explore Interactive. She also assists in preparing for and understanding interactions with external funders.

Jim Dreher Advisor

With the Silicon Valley experience of founding and launching several startups, Jim integrates his extensive knowledge of startups to advise Explore Interactive on potential emerging markets in the EdTech industry. Dr. Brenda Capobianco Educational Consultant

An esteemed researcher in STEM educational pedagogy, Dr. Capobainco has worked with over 300 teachers across Indiana to pilot a program known as Science Learning through Engineering Design (SLED). This program implements engineering principles in teaching scientific concepts.

Dr. Michelle Pittard Educational Consultant

Dr. Pittard is the Chair of the Education Studies Department at Wabash College and an expert in pedagogical teaching methods. Her most recent research projects include: examining the relationship between liberal arts education and teacher preparation and classroom-based research for pre-service teachers.
